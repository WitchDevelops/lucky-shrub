About us

Lucky Shrub is a garden design firm based in Tuscon, Arizona that specializes in creating beautiful and sustainable outdoor spaces. Our team, led by husband and wife duo Jason and Maria, is passionate about plants and dedicated to helping our clients transform their yards into oases they can be proud of. With years of experience in garden design, maintenance, and landscaping, we have the skills and expertise to bring your vision to life. In addition to our design and landscaping services, we also run a small plant nursery that sells a variety of indoor and outdoor plants, making us a one-stop shop for all your gardening needs.

Services

At Lucky Shrub, we offer a wide range of services to help you create the garden of your dreams. Our services include:

  • Garden Design
  • Landscaping
  • Maintenance

Garden Design: Our garden architect, Jason, will work with you to create a custom design that suits your style, budget, and needs. Landscaping: Our team of experienced landscapers will bring your design to life, installing everything from plants and trees to walkways and patios. Maintenance: We offer ongoing maintenance services to keep your garden looking its best, including regular pruning, fertilizing, and irrigation. Plant Nursery: In addition to our design and landscaping services, we also run a small plant nursery that sells a variety of indoor and outdoor plants.
